Convenience macro. MIDI channels are numbered from 1-10 (natural numbers) however the logical channel addresses are zero-indexed. This converts a natural MIDI channel number into the logical channel address.

Task to set the configuration of the attached device after it has been enumerated, and to read in note on/off messages from the attached MIDI device and print it to the serial port. When the board joystick or buttons are pressed, note on/off messages are sent to the attached device.
